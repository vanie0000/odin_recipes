Cheesy Ramen Noodles

Description

Melting cheese in ramen makes a quick, simple and delicious cheap meal with instant noodles and American cheese.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 package any flavor ramen noodles
  • 1 slice American cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Bring water to boil in a saucepan.
  3. Add ramen noodles and cook until tender, about 2 minutes.
  4. Pour out water.
  5. Stir in seasoning packet and cheese until blended well.
