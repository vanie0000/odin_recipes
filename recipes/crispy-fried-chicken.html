Crispy Fried Chicken

Description

The only fried chicken recipe you'll ever need. This combines all the tips and tricks to ensure the juiciest, most flavorful and the crispiest fried chicken every time.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Buttermilk
  • Flour
  • Seasonings (Paprika, Salt and Pepper)
  • Vegetable Oil

Steps

  1. Make the Breading
  2. Dredge the Chicken
  3. Fry the Chicken
  4. Drain it on paper towels.
